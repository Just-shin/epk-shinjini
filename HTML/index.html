BIOGRAPHY

Shinjini Moitra is an accomplished Hindustani classical vocalist from Ranikuthi, Kolkata, West Bengal. With 14 years of intensive training under **Guru Pampa Mukherjee**, Shinjini has mastered the traditional genres of Indian classical music.

Her unique artistry lies in her ability to blend the traditional structures of Khayal, Thumri, Nazrulgeeti, and Bhajans with a **modern, minimal aesthetic**. She is celebrated for her emotive delivery, clear tonal quality, and strong technical foundation, captivating audiences with her Soft Classical style.
